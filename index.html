About us

datasophos is a boutique data and scientific software consultancy focused on helping companies, organizations, and research laboratories leverage state of the art data methods to drive innovation and growth.

We leverage a unique blend of technical expertise and domain knowledge to help organizations unlock the full potential of their data. With a focus on collaboration, transparency, and community engagement, we specialize in designing and implementing customized solutions that leverage open-source technologies to drive business value, scientific discovery, and understanding from complex data.

Who is datasophos ?

Joshua A. Taillon, Ph.D.

Josh is a data scientist and software engineer with a firm academic foundation in materials science. He performed his Ph.D. work in Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Maryland, where he devised novel approaches to data analysis in analytical electron and ion beam microscopy. He then spent 9 years at NIST as a data science and engineering leader, publishing multiple peer-reviewed works on modernizing data management in complex research environments.

After leaving NIST, Josh founded datasophos in 2025 as a way to provide clients a way to access his expertise in more formal relationships. With datasophos , he helps clients navigate the open source landscape and builds custom solutions for their unique needs.

For more information on Josh's personal and professional interests, please visit his personal website at: https://www.joshuataillon.com.

What we offer

datasophos can help with a wide range of services in the data landscape. Our expertise is built from decades of leadership in the open source and scientific communities, and we are committed to continued advancement in these areas. While our open source work is free for anyone to use and extend, we offer bespoke consulting services on an hourly or fixed-fee basis to those looking for help doing so. Some of our areas of expertise are as follows:

Data pipelines and workflows

At datasophos , we have extensive experience building custom data pipelines to automate workflows from disparate sources. No matter the scale of your data, we can design workflows to help you gain insight, using custom solutions and/or industry standard tools such as dbt and Apache Airflow. Contact us today to learn how you can unlock the wisdom within your data via workflow engineering.

Data pipelines and workflows

Modern organizations manage data across diverse domains, and consolidating this information can reveal previously-hidden insights. If your problems need custom data workflows, we can help.

Data modeling and database design

The right data model is crucial to understanding the requirements of the task at hand, and pays dividends throughout your organization. A suitable data model can help you identify relationships, organize your data, facilitate decision making, and ensure data quality and consistency, among many other benefits. At datasophos , we are experts in modern data modeling languages such as LinkML and understand how to translate your business or research needs into a formal model or database design. Contact us today to learn how formalizing your data model can help you reach your goals.

Data modeling and database design

datasophos knows that data works best when its structure complements the needs of a given objective. Whether you are starting a greenfield project or bringing fresh eyes to existing processes, we can help.

Open source development and deployment

Today's open source ecosystem provides tools for nearly any need. While open source software is typically free to use, organizations can spend countless hours of staff time without a clear plan in place for software deployment. At datasophos , we have decades of experience using and deploying open sources solutions and can provide tailored guidance and support throughout a deployment process, ensuring seamless integration with your existing systems.

We have particular experience in deploying open-source facility and data management solutions, such as NEMO, NexusLIMS, and eLabFTW.

Additionally, we are expert Python developers, and if you have a software development task in mind, datasophos can make it happen.

Open source development and deployment

Free software powers much of what we do at datasophos and we believe strongly in the power of the open source community. If you are interested in deploying open source software or developing custom tools, we can help.

Data science, analysis, and training

The team at datasophos has a rich pedigree of scientific research experience, including advanced data analyses on highly multidimensional datasets. We specialize in using state-of-the-art open source tools to enable reproducible and traceable analyses. We can help with one-off studies or the creation of data analysis tools (dashboards, apps, etc.) that enable your team to develop their own insights.

Additionally, we are certified in software engineering instruction, and have extensive experience teaching basic and advanced software concepts to technical audiences. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help with your analyses or upskilling your organization's staff.

Data science, analysis, and training

Scientific inquiry is at the heart of datasophos and we have extensive experience applying state of the art analysis techniques to unlock wisdom from data. If you have an interesting question you need support in answering, or training on how to do so, we can help.
